This is a comb alignment.
Combe sorting is an algorithm that compares two adjacent records with a specific amount of reduction and sorts them interchangeably if they are not in order.

Characteristics of the Combe Alignment Algorithm
Advantages: 1. It is a relatively simple alignment designed to solve the frustration of bubble alignment.
Disadvantage: 1. It is not a stable sort
         2.In other words, algorithms are relatively less stable because they are not always in the same order

Time Complexity of Combe Alignment
Best : O(n^2) , Average : O(n log n) , Worst : O(n^2 / 2^p(p is the number of increases))
